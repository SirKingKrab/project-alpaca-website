Questions & Answers

  • Will there be a Multiplayer mode?

    • At this time, Multiplayer is not planned for the final release. Single player will be the main focus.

  • What platforms will I be able to play the game on?

    • The game will be available on Windows, Mac, and Linux at this time.

  • Will Project Alpaca be on *insert platform here* in the future?

    • Maybe? We don't know. Possibly? We'll see.

  • What engine is Project Alpaca made in?

    • We are using Unity 5 to make the game.

  • Where can I play Project Alpaca?

    • You can download the current build of the game right on this site or play right in the browser (Unity Supported Browsers Only).

  • I found a bug in the game? Is there a way I can let you know?

    • Send your issues to alpacadev@gmail.com.

  • Your game is bad and you should feel bad.

    • Send your complaints to alpacadev@gmail.com.

  • How much will the final game cost?

    • The game will be 100% free to play.

  • Will there be controller support?

    • We will be supporting Xbox 360, Xbox One, and Playstation 4 controllers at this time.

  • What are the system requirements needed to run this game?

    • This is hard to estimate as the game is still being developed and optimized but any computer made in the last 6-7 years should handle the game.

  • How can I learn more about the game as it progresses?

    • Checkout our Facebook, Twitter, and Tumblr pages which will give you updates on upcoming features and news.

  • I hate alpacas, can I still enjoy your game?

    • If you hate alpacas, then you must be a very sad individual but if you love platformers, you may enjoy the game.

  • I am an alpaca, can I play your game?

    • Here at Team Alpaca, we beleive that anyone can play our game, including alpacas.

  • Do you love alpacas?

    • Yes.
